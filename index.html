Rozes

Roses are beautiful flowering plants that belong to the genus Rosa. They are known for their fragrant flowers and come in various colors and varieties.

Rose Varieties

  • Red Roses: Symbolize love and romance.
  • White Roses: Represent purity and innocence.
  • Pink Roses: Convey admiration and gratitude.
  • Yellow Roses: Symbolize friendship and joy.
  • Orange Roses: Signify enthusiasm and desire.
